Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

What is the school fee structure?

Our fees are competitive and inclusive of tuition, meals, and most learning materials. Detailed fee structure is available at the school office or upon request via email.

When is the admission period?

Admissions run throughout the year, but the main intake is in January and May. Early application is highly encouraged.

Does the school offer boarding facilities?

Yes, we offer both day and boarding options with high standard hostel facilities for boys and girls.

What subjects do you offer at O'Level and A'Level?

We offer a full range of subjects including Sciences, Arts, and Vocational subjects as per the Uganda National Curriculum.

Are there scholarship opportunities?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships to outstanding and deserving students every year.

What co-curricular activities are available?

We have Football, Netball, Music, Dance, Debate, Scouting, and many more clubs and sports.
